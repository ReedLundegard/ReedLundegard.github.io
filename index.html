ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Reed and I'm currently a student of Financial Economic and Mathematics at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. My career interests include empirical economics research, financial analysis, and entrepenuership. In my free time I am practicing self-expression through music production, photography, and developing this website. My other interest include dogs, the great outdoors, and thinking big thoughts.
